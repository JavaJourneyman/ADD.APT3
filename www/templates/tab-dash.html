Welcome to ADD.APT

The purpose of "ADD.APT" is to provide users who have been diagnosed with ADD/ADHD.

Here are the top 3 reasons why you might want to begin tracking your focus:

Identify Patterns: By consistently documenting our focus, we can start to identify both positive and negative life influences on our level of focus.

Helps With Goal Setting: By identifying your focus state, you will be able to establish goals for emotional change and choose specific goals tailored to address the unfocused state.

Regaining Control: Research has shown that the process of simply checking in with current focus states can helps us feel better.

To help remind you to check in regarding how focused you are, ADD.APT will send periodic push notifications encouraging you to touch base with your level of focus.
